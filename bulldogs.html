Bulldogs

ILLUSTRATION • UNI PROJECT

Bulldogs is an illustation I made up to look like an American football team logo. At the time I was looking at a lot of the different logos and wanted to have a go at making my own.

PROJECT INFO

As I progressed through creating the illustration I thought it would possibly look good on clothing so I uploaded it to Threadless to see if anyone liked it. It got 90 votes overall which is nothing when it comes to the other ones on that site but I was happy that some people liked the look of it.

This project was really an experiment to see how I would get on with some illustration. Hopefully in the future I will be able to play about with it a bit more.
