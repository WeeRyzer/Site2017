Turbo

BRANDING • WEB • WIP

My aim for Turbo was originally to be a motor magazine including prototypes, luxury, everyday and modified vehicles. After designing the logo, I started on an app that would incorporate a social media platform and an online magazine section. I have been into cars from a young age which has led me to creating this brand.

PROJECT INFO

The logo incorporates a 2D illustration of a turbo that could potentially stand alone and be animated for use in video and loading sections.

I have selected a few screens of what the app design looks like so far showing the log in screen, menu, homepage and an online article from a verified submitter.
