ABOUT ME

RYAN DUNCAN

I'm 23, from Aberdeen, Scotland and currently in my final year studying Digital Media at RGU.

Web design and front-end development are the main topics I enjoy alongside branding and video editing. I also have experience in using 3D applications to create models, buildings and animations.

INTERESTS

Gaming has been a big part of my life for many years and I enjoy keeping up with all the latest news.

Cars are something I am also really interested in. Having a full driving licence has given me a lot of freedom. Eventually I would like to own an old VW Golf (MK1, MK2, MK3.. actually any VW would do) or a classic Mini.

I also hope to get into photography and video production more too.

SKILLS

I am confident in using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and Microsoft Office. Alongside this, I enjoy designing and coding websites with HTML, CSS and JS.

Premiere, After Effects, 3DS Max and Cinema 4D are other programs I have knowledge in through recent work experience and university.

Through my work, I have gained skills in dealing with customers and making sure they come first which has helped when working with clients on freelance projects.
